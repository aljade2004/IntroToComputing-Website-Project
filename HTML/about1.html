     Hello, I am Aj Torres, an 18-year-old from Carmen, North Cotabato. My educational journey has been a progression of challenges, triumphs, and continuous growth. From my humble beginnings at Carmen Northwest Elementary School to my current pursuit of higher education at the University of Southern Mindanao, my story reflects determination and a thirst for knowledge.

•Elementary Years:

     My academic journey began at Carmen Northwest Elementary School, where I laid the foundation for my education. Despite facing the challenges that often come with the elementary level, I graduated with a sense of accomplishment and readiness to take on the next chapter of my learning journey.

•High School Adventures:

     Moving on to Carmen National High School, I embraced the opportunities and challenges of high school life. The rigorous curriculum, coupled with extracurricular activities, molded me into a well-rounded individual. Graduating from Carmen National High School marked a significant milestone, showcasing my dedication to education and personal growth.

•Senior High School Success:

     Transitioning into senior high school at Carmen National High School-Senior High School, I delved deeper into my academic interests. The senior high level not only provided a platform for specialization but also equipped me with skills crucial for my chosen path. Graduating from senior high school opened the door to higher education, leading me to the University of Southern Mindanao.

•University Pursuits:

     Currently in my first year at the University of Southern Mindanao, I am enrolled in the Information System program. This phase of my education represents a new chapter, filled with the excitement of exploring my chosen field. The university setting offers not only academic challenges but also opportunities for personal and professional development.
